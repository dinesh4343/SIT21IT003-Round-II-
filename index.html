The genesis of the Sairam Group of Institutions goes way back to 1989 with the start of Sairam Matriculation Higher Secondary School in Chennai. Presently, the group encompasses more than twenty institutions including Schools, Engineering Institutions, Medical Colleges, Teacher Training Institutes, Polytechnic Colleges and Management School with a student strength in excess of 20,000.
